Ingredients - Serves 4

For the filling:
  • 500g mince
  • 1 onion
  • 500g passata
  • Beef stock
For the bechamel sauce:
  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 500ml milk

Enjoy a homemade family classic

Method

  1. Fry the onions and mince together until onions are softened and mince is browned.
  2. Add the beef stock and passata and leave to simmer.
  3. Start the bechamel by melting the butter and stirring in the flour until it makes a smooth paste.
  4. Slowly add the milk, stirring continuously, until all the milk is combined and you have a smooth sauce.
  5. Layer the filling, pasta sheets, and bechamel sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Bake at 200 degrees for 25 mins
