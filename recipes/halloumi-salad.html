Grilled Halloumi Salad

Home

A bodycoach staple, this healthy and filling salad is great on summer evenings.

Ingredients

Serves 2

  • 200g halloumi
  • 1 red pepper
  • 150g cooked lentils
  • Handful of cherry tomatoes
  • Handful of rocket
  • 1 lemon - juiced
  • Pinch of chilli flakes

Method

  1. Brush the sliced pepper and halloumi with oil and fry for 5 mins each side on a griddle pan.
  2. Dice the grilled peppers and combine with the other ingredients.
  3. Top with the halloumi and serve.
